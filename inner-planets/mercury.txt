Facts about Mecury:

- Mercury is the smallest planet in the solar system and the closest one to the Sun.
- A year on Mercury is very short, only about 88 Earth days long.
- Mercury has extreme temperatures, reaching about 430°C during the day and dropping to around −180°C at night.
- Mercury has almost no atmosphere, which is why it cannot trap heat.
- Mercury has no moons or rings orbiting it.
- Mercury has water ice trapped in permanently shadowed craters near its poles, even though the planet is extremely close to the Sun.
