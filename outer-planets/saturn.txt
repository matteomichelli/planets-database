Facts about Saturn:

- Saturn is the least dense planet in the solar system and would float in water if there were a pool big enough.
- Saturn has the most visible and complex ring system, made mostly of ice and rock.
- A day on Saturn is very short, lasting only about 10.7 hours.
- Saturn has over 140 known moons, including Titan, which is larger than the planet Mercury.
- Saturn is a gas giant and does not have a solid surface you could stand on.
